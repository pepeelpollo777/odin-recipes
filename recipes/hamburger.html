Hamburger Recipe

Description

A burger is a savory sandwich with a grilled beef patty, fresh lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, and condiments,

all nestled between toasted buns.

Ingredients

  • Beef patty (or alternative patty)
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Cheese
  • Pickles (Optional)
  • Onion (Optional)
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise
  • Toasted buns

Preparation

  1. Prepare the ingredients: Wash and slice the lettuce, tomatoes, onions, and pickles.
  2. Season the patty: Add salt, pepper, and any desired spices to the beef patty.
  3. Cook the patty: Grill or pan-fry the patty for 3-4 minutes per side or until fully cooked. Add cheese on top to melt.
  4. Toast the buns: Lightly toast the buns on the grill or in a pan.
  5. Assemble the burger: Spread condiments on the buns, then layer with lettuce, tomato, the cooked patty, and additional toppings like onions or pickles.
  6. Serve: Close the burger with the top bun ans enjoy!.
