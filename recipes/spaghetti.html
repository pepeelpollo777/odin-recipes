Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

Description

A classic Italian pasta dish made with simple yet flavorful ingredients like crispy pancetta, eggs

and Parmesan cheese, creating a creamy and savory sauce that clings to every strand of spaghetti.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Olive oil

Preparation

  1. Cook the spaghetti: Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup of the pasta water and drain.
  2. Cook the pancetta: In a pan, heat a bit of olive oil and fry the pancetta until crispy. Add garlic for extra flavor if desired, then remove it.
  3. Mix the sauce: In a bowl, whisk the eggs with the Parmesan cheese and a pinch of black pepper.
  4. Combine everything: Add the spaghetti to the pan with pancetta, remove from heat, and quickly mix in the egg mixture. Use reserved pasta water to adjust the sauce's creaminess.
  5. Serve: Sprinkle with extra Parmesan and black pepper before serving.
